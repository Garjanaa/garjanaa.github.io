Satsaṅga

The Circle of Seekers

Our community is a voluntary circle of practitioners. There is no single guru, no secret hierarchy, and no pressure to stay. We gather to study, chant, sit in silence, and hold each other accountable to the highest ideals described by the sages.

Six Principles

Voluntary entry only. No birth‑based eligibility. No manipulation. Philosophical honesty. No antagonism toward the living tradition. Accountability to the texts.

Who This Is For

If you feel a genuine pull toward the ancient wisdom traditions, if the description of a person of steady wisdom feels like a portrait of who you wish to become, and if you are willing to walk the path with honesty and patience — you are welcome here.

Come freely. Build together. Stay as long as the call resonates. Leave without guilt. The roar is an invitation, and a foundation stone.
